1. Launch MainApp.
2. Use "Chainrings" dropdowns to select up to 3 chainring sizes.
2. Use "Sprockets" dropdowns to select up to 12 sprocket sizes in the cassette.
4. Press "Add setup" to add another drivetrain setup.
5. Press "Plot" to display the comparison chart.
6. Press "Reset" to hide the chart and change input sizes.
